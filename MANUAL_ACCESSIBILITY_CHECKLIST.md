# Manual accessibility checklist

Do not describe the service as fully accessible until these checks are complete.

- [ ] Complete every public task using only a keyboard.
- [ ] Confirm focus is always visible and follows a logical order.
- [ ] Test at 200% and 400% zoom without horizontal scrolling at 320 CSS pixels.
- [ ] Test with Windows High Contrast Mode.
- [ ] Test with NVDA and Firefox or JAWS and Chrome.
- [ ] Confirm all errors are announced, specific and linked to their question.
- [ ] Confirm progress wording makes sense without seeing the progress bar.
- [ ] Confirm Back preserves every answer.
- [ ] Confirm no information is communicated by colour alone.
- [ ] Confirm charts make sense from their summaries and tables alone.
- [ ] Confirm reduced-motion settings remove non-essential motion.
- [ ] Test on a small touchscreen with touch targets at least 44 by 44 pixels.
- [ ] Ask people with cognitive or learning difficulties to complete the form.
- [ ] Ask testers whether wording, choices, review and confirmation are understandable.
- [ ] Record findings, fixes, remaining barriers, test date and tester consent.
