# Owner decisions required before public launch

These are governance decisions, not software defaults or legal advice.

- [ ] Confirm the legal name of the information controller.
- [ ] Confirm and monitor the privacy contact email.
- [ ] Record the lawful basis for each processing purpose.
- [ ] Approve the final contact-detail and incident retention periods.
- [ ] Name each person allowed to act as an administrator.
- [ ] Approve the procedure and response target for rights requests.
- [ ] Approve the procedure for handling a possible personal-data breach.
- [ ] Decide whether a Data Protection Impact Assessment is required.
- [ ] Review and approve the final privacy notice.
- [ ] Decide who owns the Supabase, GitHub and Netlify accounts.
- [ ] Confirm how moderation decisions will be reviewed.
- [ ] Confirm an emergency-information owner and review date.

The software provisionally removes contact details after 365 days when the
documented maintenance task is run. This must be configured after the owner
approves a period.
